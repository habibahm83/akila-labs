Preventative Health Platform

Akila offers a ready to deploy preventative care platform that can be used for general health, wellness, weight loss, and Diabetes prevention. Build a preventative care offering that can augment your products/apps with personalization driven by Akila’s Behavioral AI engine. Integrate your products/apps with SDK, Libraries and APIs, and it’s easier than ever to offer preventative health and wellness services at scale to your customers with minimal investment.

Chronic Disease Management

Chronic disease management can produce better outcomes at lower costs with personalized digital care using Akila’s plug-n-play platform. Care providers can develop personalized care plans for their patients that can be integrated with their care management systems, EMRs, and Telehealth platforms. Extend the care you provide with continuous digital presence using Akila’s Digital care delivered via Mobile SDKs and Libraries that be integrated with your existing Mobile Applications. Providers get tools, timely, actionable insights, and patient population analytics to manage their patient cohort more effectively and improving outcomes.

Telehealth

Covid has accelerated Telehealth services with an estimated quarter of healthcare spending shifting to this channel permanently. This trend offers opportunities and challenges to providers, and Akila’s platform can help you deal with them both. Increase the engagement with your customers’ beyond Telehealth sessions by providing digital health services like preventative health coaching, remote monitoring & care, physical therapy, chronic disease management and much more. Make Telehealth encounters more impactful with actionable insights and recommendations using Akila’s AI engine.
