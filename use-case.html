Chronic Disease Management

Chronic disease care management requires a holistic approach to treating the patient where it matters most - in their home. Akila’s platform extends the reach and care capabilities of providers beyond the clinical setting. The care also incorporates lifestyle changes that are required to manage chronic conditions like Diabetes, Hypertension, and other Cardiovascular disease. Akila’s Behavioral AI can deliver personalized care for patients developed by providers. The personalized care is delivered digitally 24x7 by Akila’s platform and background insights to help providers have more impactful encounters.

Remote care can be extended beyond Telehealth by using Akila’s platform to engage with the patients and providing personalized care. The care capabilities can include | Diabetes management, Cardiovascular disease, COPD, Mental Health, Aging, Allergy, GI, Obesity, and Physical Therapy.
