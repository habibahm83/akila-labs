Milk Alternatives

Soy milk may be the alternative that has been around the longest. It is the highest-protein milk alternative with 7 or more grams per cup. Because of its protein content soy milk is the best milk substitute to use in recipes. The protein in soy milk is a complete protein. That is the type of protein in animal-based products. Most plant-based products are incomplete. They are missing at least one essential amino acid. Soy milk contains compounds called soy isoflavones. They can interact with estrogen receptors. But, research cited in Nutrients does not show a health risk.
